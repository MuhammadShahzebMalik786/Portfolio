Project Summary

All-in-one Client Finder is built as a practical CLI toolkit for sales and outreach automation. It supports two tracks: a traditional business lead pipeline (LinkedIn and Google Maps to contact extraction to AI email generation and SMTP sending), and a freelance discovery path focused on Fiverr and Upwork opportunities with AI pitch generation.

The system also includes a local model fallback with Ollama, so generation can continue if cloud APIs are unavailable.

Core Features

  • LinkedIn and Google Maps lead discovery
  • Website contact extraction (emails and phones)
  • AI personalized email drafting
  • SMTP bulk sending with sent-email tracking
  • Fiverr and Upwork client discovery
  • AI sales pitch generation by niche
  • Unattended pipeline runner with cooldown logic
  • CSV outputs in a reusable data folder
